# AFRICA CLIMATE AND ENVIRONMENT FOUNDATION (ACEF)
# STRATEGIC PLAN: 2025-2026 (1-Year)

## Vision
A resilient Africa where empowered youth lead innovative solutions for climate action, environmental protection, and sustainable development, ensuring a future free from hunger and poverty.

## Mission
To mobilize and empower African youth to actively participate in the Climate, Environment, and Sustainable Development agenda, bridging the hunger and poverty gap, mitigating climate change, protecting the environment, and conserving natural resources in Africa.

## Core Values
- **Youth Leadership**: Centering young people as agents of change.
- **Innovation**: Fostering creative and context-specific solutions.
- **Collaboration**: Building strong partnerships with diverse stakeholders.
- **Impact**: Driving measurable and sustainable positive change.
- **Integrity**: Operating with transparency, accountability, and ethical principles.
- **Inclusivity**: Ensuring equitable participation and benefits for all, especially marginalized groups.

## 1-YEAR STRATEGIC PLAN: 2025-2026

### Goal 1: Enhance Youth Capacity and Engagement in Climate & Environment Action.
- **Objective 1.1**: Develop and deliver at least 3 targeted capacity-building workshops for African youth on key climate and environmental issues.
    - *Areas of Focus*: Climate change adaptation & mitigation, chemicals & waste management, natural resources conservation.
- **Objective 1.2**: Increase youth participation in ACEF's policy advocacy initiatives by 25%.
    - *Metrics*: Number of youth actively involved in drafting position papers, participating in UN side events, or contributing to advocacy campaigns.
- **Objective 1.3**: Launch an online knowledge hub/resource platform for African youth, providing accessible information on climate and environmental topics, green skills, and opportunities.

### Goal 2: Strengthen Policy Advocacy and Influence at Regional and International Levels.
- **Objective 2.1**: Submit at least 2 high-quality position papers/policy briefs to relevant UN processes (e.g., UNFCCC, UNEP-UNEA, UN Water) before their next major sessions in 2026.
- **Objective 2.2**: Secure opportunities for at least 5 youth representatives to directly engage with policymakers and decision-makers at UN conferences/summits in 2025/2026.
- **Objective 2.3**: Establish formal partnerships with at least 2 relevant regional African bodies (e.g., AU, UNEP-Africa) to amplify youth voices.

### Goal 3: Drive Tangible Impact through Pilot Projects in Key Areas of Interest.
- **Objective 3.1**: Implement at least 1 pilot project focused on climate change adaptation or mitigation in a vulnerable African community.
    - *Sub-areas*: Climate-smart agriculture, WASH resilience, or renewable energy access.
- **Objective 3.2**: Develop a framework for youth-led community initiatives focused on sustainable waste management or natural resource conservation.
- **Objective 3.3**: Conduct a needs assessment to identify opportunities for youth job creation in the green economy in at least 2 African countries.

### Goal 4: Enhance Organizational Sustainability and Visibility.
- **Objective 4.1**: Diversify funding streams by submitting at least 5 grant proposals to new donors/foundations.
- **Objective 4.2**: Increase ACEF's social media presence and engagement by 30%.
- **Objective 4.3**: Recruit and onboard 2-3 additional core volunteers (e.g., Communications Lead, Program Manager) to strengthen organizational capacity.
